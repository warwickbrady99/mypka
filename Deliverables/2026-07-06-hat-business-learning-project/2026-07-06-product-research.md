# Product Research

## Goal

Research activewear-style hats before spending much money.

You are trying to choose a first product that local sporty people would actually wear.

## First-Product Plan

Product:

- Activewear cap.

Style:

- Clean sporty design.

Colours:

- Black.
- White.
- Grey.
- Navy.
- Dark green.

Branding:

- Small embroidered or printed logo.

First test:

- 1 to 3 designs only.

Goal:

- Prove people want it before spending too much money.

## Quality Checklist

When checking activewear caps, look for:

- Lightweight material.
- Breathability.
- Comfortable fit.
- Adjustable strap or flexible sizing.
- Sweat handling.
- Stitching quality.
- Logo placement area.
- Colour options.
- Reviews with real photos.
- Return policy.

## Local Demand Research

Ask 10 to 20 local people.

Good people to ask:

- Friends.
- Family.
- Gym-goers.
- Runners.
- Football players.
- Sports club members.
- Teen and young adult activewear fans.

Questions:

- Would you wear this hat?
- What colour would you buy?
- Would you prefer a small logo or bold logo?
- Would you wear it for gym, running, football, or casual outfits?
- What price would feel fair?
- Would you buy from a local young founder if the design was good?
- What would stop you from buying it?
- Does it look like something people here would actually wear?

## Test Method Decision

Choose one after research.

| Test method | What it means | Good if | Risk |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pre-orders | People pay or reserve before the batch | Strong interest, Dad can handle payments correctly | Must be careful and honest about delivery timing |
| Samples first | Order 1 or 2 samples before selling | You need to check quality | Costs money before sales |
| Small first batch | Order a small number to sell locally | You have clear interest | You could be left with unsold stock |
