# Research Checklist

Use this checklist before spending serious money. Save findings in this file or in a new supplier comparison document.

## Supplier Research

- [ ] Find UK-friendly custom cap suppliers.
- [ ] Check whether each supplier ships within the UK.
- [ ] Check whether each supplier works with individuals or only businesses.
- [ ] Check whether a parent or guardian needs to order because the owner is under 18.
- [ ] Record contact details and website links.

## Print-On-Demand vs Bulk Ordering

- [ ] Compare print-on-demand options.
- [ ] Compare bulk custom cap suppliers.
- [ ] Note the pros and cons of each.
- [ ] Check whether the supplier handles shipping to customers.
- [ ] Check whether products can be ordered one at a time.

Beginner note: print-on-demand can be safer because it avoids holding stock, but quality and profit margins may be lower. Bulk can be cheaper per cap, but it is riskier if the designs do not sell.

## Costs and Minimums

- [ ] Minimum order quantity.
- [ ] Sample cost.
- [ ] Embroidery setup or digitizing cost.
- [ ] Price per cap at small quantities.
- [ ] Shipping cost to you.
- [ ] Delivery time.
- [ ] Any VAT or tax shown at checkout.

## Quality Checks

- [ ] Photos of previous embroidered caps.
- [ ] Reviews from real customers.
- [ ] Stitch quality.
- [ ] Whether small text is readable.
- [ ] Thread colour options.
- [ ] Cap material and fit.
- [ ] Whether samples can be ordered before a larger order.

## Policies

- [ ] Return policy.
- [ ] Remake policy for faulty embroidery.
- [ ] Cancellation policy.
- [ ] Whether custom products are non-refundable.
- [ ] Who pays shipping if there is a problem.

## Selling Platform Research

- [ ] Shopify.
- [ ] Etsy.
- [ ] TikTok Shop.
- [ ] Instagram.
- [ ] Depop.
- [ ] eBay.
- [ ] Local sales through friends and family.

For each platform, check:

- [ ] Age rules.
- [ ] Whether a parent or guardian is required.
- [ ] Fees.
- [ ] Payment setup.
- [ ] Delivery options.
- [ ] Refund and return expectations.
- [ ] Whether it fits custom caps.

## UK Under-18 Business Rules To Research

Ask a parent or guardian to help research this before selling.

- [ ] Whether an under-18 can open the needed selling accounts.
- [ ] Whether a parent or guardian needs to own or supervise accounts.
- [ ] Payment processor age rules.
- [ ] Bank account requirements.
- [ ] Tax basics for small selling.
- [ ] Consumer rights for online sales.
- [ ] Rules around returns for customized products.
- [ ] Use of copyrighted logos, football badges, brands, music artists, or characters.

Safety rule: do not use copyrighted logos, team badges, cartoon characters, celebrity names, or other brands unless you have clear permission.
