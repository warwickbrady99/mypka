# One-Pan Lemon Herb Chicken

A weekend [[mediterranean-cooking]] main that leaves one pan to wash. Bone-in
thighs, lemon, and rosemary roasted together until the skin crisps.

## Ingredients

For 2 people:

- 4 bone-in chicken thighs
- 1 lemon (half juiced, half sliced)
- 4 cloves garlic, smashed
- 2 sprigs rosemary
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1 small red onion, wedged
- salt, pepper

## Method

1. Heat the oven to 200 C.
2. Toss everything in the roasting pan; arrange the thighs skin-side up.
3. Roast 35-40 minutes until the skin is deep golden and the juices run clear.
4. Rest 5 minutes; spoon the pan juices over before serving.
