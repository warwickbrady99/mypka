# Hat Business Learning Project

This folder is the working home for your local activewear hat business project.

Start here:

1. Start with [[2026-07-06-first-5-hat-design-ideas]].
2. Sketch the 5 activewear hat design ideas.
3. Ask 10 to 20 local people what they would actually wear.
4. Write down the feedback before doing supplier, website, tax, or business registration work.
5. Later, return to [[2026-07-06-next-actions-checklist]] with Dad's support.

## Important

This is a real selling project, but keep the first test small. Dad handles adult accounts, payment setup, supplier accounts, official/legal/tax parts, and final approval before spending money. Your focus is design, local demand research, branding, and learning how the business works.
