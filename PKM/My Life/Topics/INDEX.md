# Topics - Index

Interest areas you actively follow — AI tooling, sourdough, longevity research. The signal layer of your life: where you notice patterns before they are worth promoting. A new interest shows up here first.

## The promotion path

A Topic is a starting point, not a permanent home. When a Topic crystallizes into a measurable pursuit you intend to drive, it **graduates into a Key Element** — and only then can a Goal anchor to it. Don't just keep deepening a Topic that has clearly become a pursuit; promote it.

Example: *French* starts here as a Topic — something you are reading about and dabbling in. When it sharpens into a measurable pursuit ("reach B2 fluency"), it graduates into a [[PKM/My Life/Key Elements/INDEX|Key Element]]. Only after that promotion can a Goal anchor to it. A Goal can never anchor to a Topic directly.

## Keep it shallow and focused

Topics are the signal layer, so keep them shallow: roughly **three levels deep** at most before a sub-interest is either its own Topic or noise. Revisit the set **quarterly** — promote what has crystallized, prune what has gone quiet, and pick the handful of Topics that get your real attention this quarter. A sprawling Topic list is a sign you are collecting interests instead of following them.
