> ## ⚠ READ BEFORE YOU INSTALL — back up your folder first
>
> **1. Back up everything first (required).**
> Installing the Cockpit lets your own AI assistant **change files in your
> knowledge-base / PKA folder** — it may rewrite frontmatter, move or restructure
> files, and create or modify a database/index file — so that your content fits
> the Cockpit interface. **Before you start, make a complete backup of your
> entire folder** (copy it somewhere safe, or commit and push it to version
> control). Do not install without a backup you can restore from. If the install
> asks to modify your files and you have not backed up, stop and back up first.
>

> You operate your own LLM/AI assistant against your own files. **You alone
> decide what it does to your data.** You are responsible for reviewing its

> you. We cannot see, approve, or undo what your assistant does on your machine.

> on your normal login. But if you run the bridge in any automated, scheduled,
> or unattended way, that path must authenticate with an **Anthropic API key**
