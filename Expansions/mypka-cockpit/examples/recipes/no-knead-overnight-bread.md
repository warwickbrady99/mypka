# No-Knead Overnight Bread

Four ingredients, no kneading, one bowl. The work is the wait: an overnight rise
does what your hands would. Active time is short; total time below is hands-on
plus the bake (the 12-hour rest is uncounted).

## Ingredients

- 400 g bread flour
- 320 ml water (room temperature)
- 8 g salt
- 1 g instant yeast

## Method

1. Mix everything into a shaggy dough. Cover; rest 12-18 hours at room temp.
2. Fold the dough onto itself a few times, shape into a round, rest 45 minutes.
3. Bake in a preheated covered pot at 230 C: 30 minutes covered, 15 uncovered.
4. Cool fully on a rack before slicing.

## Notes

- The wetter the dough, the more open the crumb. Resist adding flour.
- A heavy lidded pot traps steam for the crust; no pot, add a tray of water.
