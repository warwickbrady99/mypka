## Example Pricing Formula

```text
Total cost per cap =
cap blank cost
+ embroidery or printing cost
+ shipping to me
+ packaging
+ selling platform fees
+ delivery to customer
+ other costs

Selling price =
total cost per cap + target profit per cap
```

## Beginner Rules

- Do not guess costs when taking real orders.
- Include delivery and platform fees.
- Keep proof of what you paid.
- Ask a parent or guardian to help with payments, tax basics, and account rules.
- If the price seems too high, reduce costs or simplify the product before selling at a loss.
