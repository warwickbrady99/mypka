## What Each Column Means

- Subject: the school subject name.
- Qualification type: GCSE, BTEC, vocational award, certificate, or another school qualification.
- Exam board: for example AQA, Pearson Edexcel, OCR, WJEC/Eduqas, NCFE, or another provider.
- Course / specification: the exact course title or specification code if known.
- Tier: Foundation or Higher where relevant. Maths and Science often need this checked carefully.
- Teacher / class: optional, but helpful when asking follow-up questions.
- Confirmation source: where the detail came from, such as a teacher email, school options booklet, exam timetable, specification PDF, revision guide, or official website.
- Status: use clear wording such as `confirmed from Neston website`, `needs teacher confirmation`, `needs tier confirmation`, or `needs personal route confirmation`.

## Good Questions To Ask School

- Which exam board am I doing for this subject?
- Is it GCSE or another qualification type?
- What is the exact specification or course code?
- Am I entered for Foundation or Higher tier?
- Which papers, units, or modules will I sit?
- Is there coursework, controlled assessment, or practical work?
- Which revision guide does the department recommend?
