# Habits - Index

Recurring rhythms with a cadence and a definition of done. A Habit is not a Goal (no finish line), not a Project (no scope), and not a streak (no number to optimize). It maintains; it does not end.

This folder is a **thinking space about the rhythm, not a tracker.** A Habit file holds why the rhythm exists, what it sustains, and how to tell it is working — not a log of check-marks. The streak lives in your head or your habit app; this file holds the meaning.

## The carrier role

A Habit is one of the two shapes a Goal's carrier can take — the sibling of a Project. A Project carries a Goal when the work has a finish line; a Habit carries it when the work is an open-ended rhythm. A Goal names **one** carrier, so a Habit that carries a Goal is that Goal's sole vehicle.

Example: the Goal *"lose 20 kg"* can be carried by a Habit (3 workouts a week, ongoing) **or** by a Project (an 8-week program) — never both. When a Habit is the carrier, it is the whole engine.
