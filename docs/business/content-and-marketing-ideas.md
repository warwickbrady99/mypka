# Content And Marketing Ideas

## Main Strategy

Use content to test interest before spending too much money. Show the process, ask for opinions, and let people help choose the first designs.

## TikTok Ideas

- "Starting a cap brand at 15" progress videos.
- Design poll: "Which cap would you wear?"
- Cap colour battle: black vs navy vs stone.
- Embroidery sample close-up.
- Packing a first order.
- What I learned from ordering my first sample.
- Rating my own cap designs honestly.
- Turning a sketch into a cap mockup.
- Asking people at school which design they prefer, only if everyone is comfortable being filmed.

## Instagram Ideas

- Story polls for colours.
- Story polls for logo placements.
- Reels showing design progress.
- Carousel of 3 cap concepts.
- Countdown to sample arrival.
- Close-up photos of embroidery.
- Simple outfit photos with the cap.
- Behind-the-scenes highlights.

## Behind-The-Scenes Content

- Sketching designs.
- Choosing colours.
- Comparing cap types.
- Opening sample packages.
- Checking stitching quality.
- Testing outfits.
- Learning what went wrong and what improved.

## Polls For Design Feedback

Poll examples:

- Which colour should drop first?
- Front logo or side logo?
- Big text or small logo?
- Black cap or cream cap?
- Would you wear this?
- What price feels fair?

Good feedback question:

```text
Would you actually buy this cap for £___?
```

That is more useful than asking only whether people like it.

## Launch Countdown Posts

- 7 days: show the chosen design.
- 5 days: show close-up detail.
- 3 days: show how many are available.
- 2 days: answer common questions.
- 1 day: remind people of launch time.
- Launch day: clear photo, price, how to buy, delivery estimate.

## Simple Product Photo Ideas

- Cap on a plain background near a window.
- Cap worn with a simple outfit.
- Close-up of embroidery.
- Side angle showing shape.
- Back strap detail.
- Packaging photo.
- Comparison of colour options.

Photo tips:

- Use natural light.
- Keep the background tidy.
- Show the cap clearly.
- Do not over-edit colours.

## How To Test Demand Before Spending Too Much

- Post mockups and ask people to vote.
- Ask people to join a waitlist or message if interested.
- Take no money until you know the legal/payment setup is safe.
- Order 1 to 3 samples before ordering a batch.
- Start with a tiny batch or pre-order only with parent or guardian help.
- Track who says they would buy and who actually follows through.
