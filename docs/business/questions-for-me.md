# Questions For Me

Answer these when ready. Short answers are fine.

1. What vibe do I want: streetwear, sporty, luxury, funny, local, gaming, minimalist, or something else?
2. What colours do I like for the first caps?
3. Do I already have a brand name idea?
4. Do I want embroidered text, logos, symbols, custom names, initials, or numbers?
5. What budget range is realistic for samples?
6. Do I have parent or guardian help for payments, orders, selling accounts, and legal questions?
7. Who do I imagine buying the first caps?
8. Would I rather start with one design, or test 3 to 5 concepts first?
9. Do I want the brand to feel personal to me, local to my area, or more general?
10. What brands, creators, or styles do I like, without copying them?
